### 5. demetci2022scot
**Demetci, Pinar, Rebecca Santorella, Björn Sandstede, William Stafford Noble, and Ritambhara Singh. "SCOT: Single-Cell Multi-Omics Alignment with Optimal Transport." *Journal of Computational Biology* 29, no. 1 (2022): 3–18.**

**Annotation**: SCOT introduces an unsupervised algorithm using Gromov-Wasserstein optimal transport to align single-cell multi-omics datasets without requiring paired measurements. It constructs k-NN graphs from each modality and finds a probabilistic coupling minimizing the Gromov-Wasserstein distance, with a self-tuning heuristic for hyperparameter selection. SCOT is a direct methodological predecessor to MOSAIC, which extends OT-based alignment with information-theoretic uncertainty quantification.
**Verification**: ✅ Title, all 5 authors (Demetci, Santorella, Sandstede, Noble, Singh) in correct order, journal, vol 29 no 1 pp 3–18, year 2022 confirmed via PubMed PMID 35050714 and Semantic Scholar.
